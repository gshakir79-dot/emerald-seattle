EMERALD/Day Trips

04 — Beyond the City

Day trips worth the drive

Six escapes, each with an hour-by-hour plan, a map, and the honest logistics nobody prints on the brochure.

2.5 HR DRIVEFULL DAY

Mount Rainier National Park

The mountain that photobombs the skyline, up close. Paradise wildflower meadows, glacier views, and pie on the way home.
35 MIN FERRYHALF DAY

Bainbridge Island

Walk on the ferry from downtown — the crossing IS the attraction. Bookstores, bakeries, and a golden-hour sail home into the skyline.
40 MIN DRIVE2–3 HOURS

Snoqualmie Falls

A 268-foot curtain of water — taller than Niagara — twenty-five minutes from downtown. Twin Peaks fans, this is that lodge.
2 HR DRIVEFULL DAY

Leavenworth

A Bavarian village in the Cascades, committed to the bit since 1962. Bratwurst, beer halls, river floats, and a magical lights season.
3–4 HR + FERRYLONG DAY / OVERNIGHT

San Juan Islands

Orcas in the wild, lavender farms, and harbor towns on island time. Doable in a long day; better as an overnight.
FERRY + 2 HRFULL DAY MINIMUM

Olympic Peninsula

Three worlds in one park: mile-high ridge views, a mossy rainforest cathedral, and wild Pacific beaches. Pick one per day.
