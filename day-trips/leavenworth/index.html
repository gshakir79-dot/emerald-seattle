Beyond the City — 2 HR DRIVE · FULL DAY

Leavenworth

In 1962, a dying timber town bet everything on becoming a Bavarian village — architecture codes, lederhosen, the works. Sixty years on, the bit is the town, the Alps-adjacent scenery holds up its end, and the bratwurst is legitimately good.

✓ Field-checked July 2026

The Plan

8:00

Drive US-2

The Stevens Pass route is the scenic argument: Skykomish River, granite walls, and Deception Falls a two-minute leg-stretch off the highway.

10:30

Front Street

The full Bavarian streetscape. München Haus for brats and a liter on the biergarten deck; the Gingerbread Factory for the kitsch course.

13:00

Pick your afternoon

Summer: float the Wenatchee River or walk the Icicle Gorge loop. Shoulder season: wine tasting rooms line the village. Connoisseurs of the absurd: the Nutcracker Museum has over 7,000 of them.

17:00

Dinner and the drive home

Or stay for the lights — from Thanksgiving through February the village glows nightly, and it is genuinely, unironically magical.

The MapTap a marker for directions

Need to know

  • December weekends are mobbed — hotels book out months ahead and Front Street becomes a slow shuffle. Go midweek for lights with elbow room.
  • Loop option: go out via US-2, return via Blewett Pass and I-90 to make it a circle drive.
  • Winter travel over Stevens Pass can require chains — check WSDOT pass reports before leaving.
